Designing a network in Packet Tracer to connect Sales and Marketing departments .

Requirements: 
a)Each department should have at least 2 Pcs 
b)Appropriate number of switches and routers should be used in the network 
c)Using the given network address 192.168.40.0 , all interfaces should be configured with appropriate IP addresses , subnet mask and gateway 
d)All devices in the network should be connected using appropriate cables 
e)Test connectivity between Sales and Marketing departments - PCs in Sales should be able to ping PCs in the Marketing department.
